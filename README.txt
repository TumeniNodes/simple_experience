===Simple Experience Mod for MineTest===
by Alex Carroll and Andres Lucca

To check your experience (and level) simply type '/myxp' into the chat. 

The following blocks give this amount of experience:
	Coal - 3
	Copper - 5
	Iron - 10
	Gold - 15
	Diamond - 20
	Mese - 30
